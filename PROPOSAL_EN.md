### 3. Introducing "Kioku": A New AI Feature for LINE

When integrated into LINE, "Kioku" will address the core problems that journaling users face: **"information overload"** and **"lost connections"** with their own past experiences.

> ðŸ’¡ **User Problem Details**: See the [Problem - Information Overload](./INTRODUCE_BUSINESS_EN.md#problem---information-overload) section in the product documentation to better understand the pain points users are experiencing.

"Kioku" operates based on a unique technological approach:

1.  **Extract Meaning:** When users write journal entries in LINE, AI (via Cloud LLM API) automatically identifies and categorizes entities (people, places, emotions, topics).
2.  **Discover Connections:** AI then establishes contextual relationships between these entities (e.g., "Work" *CAUSES* "Stress"; "Sarah" *REDUCES* "Stress").
3.  **Provide Deep Insights:** A **Personalized Knowledge Graph** is formed, becoming the AI's "long-term memory". This allows users to chat with AI and receive deep, personalized answers with concrete evidence from their own journal.
